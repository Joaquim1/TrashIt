How It Works

You let us know which 2 days every week we should pickup your trash bags on and what time they should be picked up.
Once your selected day comes, you place your trash bags outside your front door.
Within the 1-hour time slot you have chosen, a trash-collector will come to pickup your trash and take it to a dumpster.
