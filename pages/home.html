What We Do
Two times a week, we send a trash-collector to your home to pickup trash bags from your front door.

How It Works

You Tell Us When

  • While subscribing to our service, you tell us what 2 days to pickup your trash.
  • For each of those days, you pick an hour time slot in which a trash-collector will come to get it.
  • Once subscribed, you can easily make changes to your subscription if need be

Trash Full?

  • Once your selected days come, place your trash bags outside your front door during your time slot
  • This way, you will not be charged by your apartment complex for having trash outside
  • We recommend you place them outside 10 minutes before the timeslot

Trash Pickup

  • A trash collector will pickup your trash-bags from your front door
  • This way, its quick, easy, and your home stays clean
